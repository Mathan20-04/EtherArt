About Us


We are Entrepreneurs, Artists, Engineers, and Farmers celebrating the '75th anniversary of India’s Independence'. We have spent the last year designing unique digital collectables (NFTs) that showcase India’s victories so all of us can share them with the world on this special occasion!

For thousands of years, India has given precious gifts to humankind, including Yoga and the Number Zero…even the game of Chess was born here!

Without these marvels, the world today wouldn’t be the same. Fast forward to this century, under the leadership of Prime Minister Modiji, the country has united to achieve unthinkable social and economic progress for India’s 1.3 billion population.


There is no better example than the 200+ crores (2 Billion+) Covid vaccinations administrated as of July 2022 by healthcare workers who reached remote areas such as Nagaland, Manipur, Andaman and Nicobar, trekking for hours on foot (and on ponies!) to take care of nomadic tribes in the mountains. We salute the healthcare workers of India!

These victories stretch to the farmland where 9 crores (90 million) clean cooking gas connections, such as LPG, were made available to rural households which were otherwise using traditional cooking fuels such as firewood, coal and cow-dung cakes.

And across India, bank accounts have been set up for over 45 crores (450 million) people who previously never had one – a giant leap for financial inclusion!

We are proud to create NFTs to celebrate India’s historic social and economic victories. Today we are releasing our first NFT collection “200+ Crore Vaccination” to honor our country’s fight against the pandemic.

Each NFT is unique. You could collect these NFTs and pass them onto your next generation, or trade them should you desire!
