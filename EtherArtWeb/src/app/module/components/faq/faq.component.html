FAQs


1) What is an NFT?

NFTs mean non-fungible tokens on blockchain with unique identification codes which mean each NFT (digital collectable) offered by EtherArt is unique.


2) Do I need to purchase a cryptocurrency to buy EtherArt NFTs?

No. All purchases and sale are using either INR and USD.

 

3) How do I buy an NFT ?

You can buy an NFT by paying in INR or equivalent USD. All the prices shown on the card is in INR. You can pay through your Credit Card, Net Banking, UPI, Debit Card.

 

4) How do I sell an NFT ?

By default, all the NFTs owned by you will be NOT FOR SALE. If you want to sell it, to http://www.etherart.ai/myaccount , click on “Collectables”, click on “Ask” to set your price for the other users to purchase. Other users can see your Item listed for Sale and can buy your NFT.

 

5) How do I redeem my profit earned from trading NFTs?

Customer Payout is amount of Profit you make selling your NFT in secondary market. Profit is equal to sale price of NFT you own minus purchase price you paid to buy the NFT minus any applicable taxes and commission. By using the Site or the Marketplace Offerings, you agree that to the following:

A)   If you are a customer living in India with +91 mobile number, you are eligible to request a Customer Payout as long as your EtherArt account balance is more than Rs.100.

B)   For other customers outside of India who have paid in United States Dollar, you are eligible for you request for a Customer Payout if your account balance is more than US $100.

C)   Your Customer Payout will be processed in 10 business days.

9) What is EtherScan?

Etherscan is a block explorer and analytics platform that allows you to access details on Ethereum. You NFT contract is stored in Ethereum and it shows the authenticity of your unique NFT.

 

10) Can I download my NFT on my phone or computer?

Yes, you can download your NFT on your phone or computer. However, the images downloaded is not original. Though you can download the authenticity of owner that is stored in the block-chain.
